📋 A Few Things to Keep in Mind

👟We kindly ask that you remove your shoes at the entrance — a shoe rack with complimentary slippers is waiting for you just inside the door
🚭Please keep the unit smoke-free. There's a designated outdoor area if needed. We appreciate your understanding — it helps keep the space fresh for everyone.
🌙Quiet hours begin at 10:00 PM. We're nestled in a warm residential community — a little consideration for the neighbors goes a long way, and we truly appreciate it.
🏠No gatherings or parties, please. Cascade is a peaceful retreat — only registered guests are welcome inside to preserve the calm for everyone nearby.
👥Only guests included in your booking may stay overnight. If your plans change and your group grows, just let us know ahead of time — we're happy to sort it out.
💡When stepping out, please switch off the A/C, lights, and appliances — small habits that make a big difference, and we're grateful for your mindfulness.
💧Kindly use the bin provided for tissue, toilet paper, and sanitary items — not the toilet. Our plumbing is a little sensitive and we want to keep everything flowing smoothly for you and future guests.
🗑️If the indoor bins fill up, tie the bags and leave them in the large outdoor bin provided — or just message us and we'll arrange pickup. No trouble at all.
✨Please help us care for the space. If anything breaks or seems off, let us know right away — no judgment, things happen, and we'd rather know so we can fix it for you.
